Registration

To register a child at Spring Lake Elementary, please bring the following items to the registrar from 8:00 a.m. - 3:30 p.m. Monday through Friday.

- Birth Certificate

- Social Security Card

- A Valid HRS 680 Florida certificate of Immunization - original required.

- Report Card - for grade verification if applicable

School Advisory

What are the responsibilities of SAC members?

1) Every year schools receive money for every student from the state of Florida. SAC decides where and how to spend this money.

2) It reviews and revises the School Improvement Plan annually.

3) It makes decision that affect campus wide policies and procedures.

4) It promotes parent and school interaction and communication.

5) It addresses areas of concern and suggest ways to improve existing conditions.
